Glow & Grace Skincare

Premium Pakistani Skincare Brand

Transforming a struggling skincare brand into a profitable e-commerce powerhouse through strategic Meta ads optimization and signal-based scaling.

Project Duration: 5 Months
Completion Date: April 2025
Initial ROAS: 0.5x
Final ROAS: 3.2x

Project Overview

Glow & Grace Skincare approached me with a struggling Meta ads account that was generating a negative return on ad spend (ROAS). Despite having high-quality products and positive customer reviews, their digital marketing efforts were failing to deliver profitable results.

Over a 5-month period, I completely restructured their Meta ads strategy, implemented proper pixel tracking, and developed a signal-based scaling system that transformed their business. The results were dramatic: from PKR 2.7M in revenue in the first month to PKR 50M by month 5, all while maintaining profitability.

The Challenge

Initial Problems Identified

  • Fragmented Campaign Structure: Over 25 small ad sets with minimal budgets, preventing Meta's algorithm from gathering sufficient data.

  • Improper Pixel Implementation: Basic pixel setup without custom events, leading to poor signal quality and ineffective optimization.

  • Audience Overlap: Multiple ad sets targeting similar audiences, causing internal competition and increased costs.

  • Ineffective Creative Strategy: Generic product images without clear value propositions or compelling calls-to-action.

  • No Scaling Framework: Arbitrary budget increases without data-driven decision making or systematic approach.

When Glow & Grace approached me, they were facing several critical business challenges due to their ineffective Meta ads strategy:

Key Strategic Elements

Advanced Pixel Implementation

Implemented server-side tracking with Conversion API and custom events to capture micro-conversions throughout the customer journey, significantly improving signal quality.

Simplified Account Structure

Consolidated 25+ ad sets into 3 core campaigns with logical structure: Prospecting, Retargeting, and Loyalty, eliminating audience overlap and competition.

Custom Audience Strategy

Created value-based lookalike audiences and segmented retargeting audiences based on engagement level and purchase intent signals.

Signal-Based Scaling System

Developed a proprietary scaling framework based on real-time performance signals, allowing for systematic budget increases while maintaining profitability.

Challenges & Learnings

Key Challenges

iOS 14+ Privacy Changes

Apple's privacy updates significantly impacted data visibility, requiring implementation of server-side tracking and alternative measurement solutions to maintain accurate performance data.

Seasonal Demand Fluctuations

Skincare purchasing patterns varied significantly during different seasons and holidays, requiring adaptive budget allocation and creative strategies to maintain consistent performance.

Inventory Management

Rapid scaling created inventory challenges, requiring close coordination between marketing and operations to prevent stockouts and maintain customer satisfaction during high-demand periods.

Competitive Market

The Pakistani skincare market became increasingly competitive during the project, requiring continuous creative refreshes and unique value propositions to maintain market share.

Key Learnings & Best Practices

Signal Quality Over Quantity

Investing in proper tracking infrastructure and focusing on high-quality signals proved more valuable than generating large volumes of low-quality data. This approach allowed for more accurate optimization and better algorithmic learning.

Systematic Scaling Approach

Developing a clear, data-driven framework for scaling decisions eliminated guesswork and emotional decision-making, allowing for consistent growth while maintaining profitability even during challenging periods.

Creative-First Strategy

In the competitive skincare market, creative quality and messaging proved to be the primary differentiator. Continuous testing and iteration of ad creatives based on performance data was essential for maintaining strong results.

Cross-Functional Collaboration

Success required close coordination between marketing, operations, and customer service teams. Establishing clear communication channels and shared KPIs across departments was crucial for managing the rapid growth.
